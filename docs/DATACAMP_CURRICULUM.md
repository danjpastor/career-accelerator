## Important limitation

Career Accelerator does not have access to the user's private DataCamp account,
so it cannot detect the exact exercise currently open on DataCamp. It assigns
the next official chapter based on chapters marked complete in Career
Accelerator.

DataCamp can revise course content. When that occurs, the fixed curriculum
catalog should be re-verified and updated; the adaptive scheduling engine does
not need to change.
