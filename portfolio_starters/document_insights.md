## Writing test

Each finding should answer:

- What happened?
- Compared with what?
- By how much?
- For which segment or period?
- Why might it matter?
- What can and cannot be concluded?

Avoid vague phrases such as “significant improvement” unless the magnitude or statistical test is provided.
