# Applied Lab 24: Calculate confidence intervals and margin of error

**Category:** Statistics
**Roadmap week:** 6
**Estimated time:** 55 minutes
**Concepts:** standard error, confidence level, confidence interval, margin of error, sample size


## Objective

Estimate uncertainty around a sample mean and proportion and communicate what a confidence interval does and does not mean.

## Steps

1. Calculate a sample mean, standard deviation, and standard error.
2. Build a 95% confidence interval for mean response time.
3. Build a confidence interval for a conversion proportion.
4. Compare interval width for two sample sizes.
5. Write a stakeholder-friendly interpretation without claiming a probability about the fixed parameter.

## Deliverables

- Runnable calculations.
- Confidence-interval comparison table.
- Plain-language interpretation.
