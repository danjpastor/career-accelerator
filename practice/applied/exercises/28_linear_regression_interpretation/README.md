# Applied Lab 28: Fit and interpret a simple linear regression

**Category:** Statistics
**Roadmap week:** 10
**Estimated time:** 60 minutes
**Concepts:** dependent variable, predictor, slope, intercept, residual, R-squared, extrapolation, coefficient interpretation


## Objective

Fit a basic regression, validate its assumptions at an introductory level, and explain the coefficient without making a causal claim.

## Steps

1. Define the outcome and predictor in business terms.
2. Fit a simple linear model.
3. Interpret slope, intercept, and R-squared.
4. Inspect residual patterns and influential observations.
5. Explain why prediction outside the observed range is risky.

## Deliverables

- Runnable model.
- Coefficient and fit summary.
- Plain-language interpretation and limitations.
