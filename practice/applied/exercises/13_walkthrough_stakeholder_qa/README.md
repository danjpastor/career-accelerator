# Applied Lab 13: Prepare a dashboard walkthrough and stakeholder Q&A

**Category:** Communication
**Roadmap week:** 9
**Estimated time:** 45 minutes
**Concepts:** five-minute walkthrough, narrative order, likely questions, evidence-based answers

## Objective

Explain a dashboard in five minutes and prepare for likely manager questions.

## Steps

1. Write a five-minute audience-problem-evidence-action-limitations outline.
2. Choose the three visuals to show first.
3. Draft three likely stakeholder questions.
4. Prepare concise evidence-based answers.
5. Rehearse and revise.

## Deliverables

- Timed walkthrough outline.
- Three questions and answers.
- Self-review.
