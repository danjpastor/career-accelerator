# Applied Lab 18: Repair a misleading analytical story

**Category:** Broken Analysis
**Roadmap week:** 6
**Estimated time:** 45 minutes
**Concepts:** chart scale, cherry-picking, average of averages, annotation, uncertainty

## Objective

Identify how a chart and narrative exaggerate a change and rebuild them with context.

## Steps

1. List misleading visual and statistical choices.
2. Recalculate selectively summarized metrics.
3. Choose an appropriate scale and period.
4. Rewrite the headline.
5. Add a meaningful limitation or annotation.

## Deliverables

- Corrected chart or redesign.
- Corrected headline and paragraph.
- List of misleading choices.
